Jackie Chan

Martial artist, actor, beloved cultural icon

Jackie Chan is one of the world's most recognizable faces in cinema, known for his decades of acrobatic self-performed stunts as well as his dedication to the action-comedy genre.

Martial Arts

At the young age of 6, Jackie trained at the China Drama Academy, where he excelled at martial arts and acrobatics. He has mastered the following martial arts styles:
  • Northern and Southern Style Kung Fu
  • Hapkido
  • Karate
  • Judo
  • Taekwondo
  • Jeet Kune Do

“The ads all call me fearless, but that’s just publicity. Anyone who thinks I’m not scared out of my mind whenever I do one of my stunts is crazier than I am.” – Jackie Chan
